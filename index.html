Our Journey

What started out with just an idea to build a luggage logistics platform connecting travellers to local shops and businesses for temporary storage has evolved to include a dash service enabling merchants in Singapore for their deliveries due the COVID-19 pandemic had greatly limited the movement of people and goods around the country.

Accredited by Enterprise Singapore, Travelhorse now is now part of the Food Delivery Booster Package to support F&B businesses making the transition from offline to online sales.
