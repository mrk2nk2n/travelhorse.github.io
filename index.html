Our Journey

What started out with just an idea to build a luggage logistics platform connecting travellers to local shops and businesses for temporary storage has evolved to include a dash service enabling merchants in Singapore for their deliveries due the COVID-19 pandemic had greatly limited the movement of people and goods around the country.

Accredited by Enterprise Singapore, Travelhorse now is now part of the Food Delivery Booster Package to support F&B businesses making the transition from offline to online sales.

Covid-19 Update

In light of the Covid-19, the team at Travelhorse believes in rallying local startups and SMEs to come together to service Singapore. We launched F&B Dash and Logistics Delivery services for our partner merchants and are now opening the service to all companies in Singapore. To further support those in need, our team is extending our logistic services to cover Packing & Storage for any students, professionals or small business owners who has been displaced by the virus or due to the "Circuit Breaker".

If you are a startup, a company or just an individual looking for an Island-wide delivery, packing or storage services, we are able to help. Click on the buttons to choose the service, submit your details in the Airtable form and we will get back to you within an hour.

For urgent bookings, please contact us at hello@travelhorse.co
